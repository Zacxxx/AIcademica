## Title

AIcademica

An AI Agent University for real people.

<details>
<summary><strong>One-line pitch</strong></summary>

AIcademica accelerates learning by turning education into a structured institution of specialized agents.

</details>

## The Problem

- Learning is often fragmented.
- People struggle to turn curiosity into a structured path.
- Assessment comes too late, or not at all.
- Teaching tools are rarely designed around actual mastery.

## The Thesis

- Accelerate learning with specialized agents.
- Keep education conventional enough to be trustworthy.
- Separate planning, teaching, assessment, and supervision.
- Make the learning path measurable from the start.

## The University Model

- Supervisor agents set the season.
- Educational body agents create programs and classes.
- Professor agents teach async.
- Examination agents verify learning.
- Treasurer agents keep the institution funded.

<details>
<summary><strong>Agent responsibilities</strong></summary>

- **Supervisor** sets direction.
- **Educational body** turns direction into curriculum.
- **Professor** teaches and explains.
- **Examination** checks mastery.
- **Treasurer** protects viability.

</details>

## How Teaching Works

1. The season is defined.
2. The program is created before classes begin.
3. The program is injected into the professor agent.
4. The teacher develops it and teaches with structure.
5. Students ask questions and get explanations in context.

## How Verification Works

- Assignments are created by examination agents.
- Tests verify what students actually learned.
- IRL events can be organized when in-person validation matters.
- Grades and certifications become durable progress signals.

## The Student Outcome

- Students choose a diploma target.
- They apply to the classes required for that diploma.
- They collect grades and certifications.
- They graduate with evidence of acquired knowledge.

<details>
<summary><strong>What success looks like</strong></summary>

Students do not just “finish” content. They leave with a verified learning trail that maps to a real diploma goal.

</details>

## System Stack

- Next.js for the frontend
- Bun for the project runtime and package management
- Rust for core services and orchestration
- Supabase for auth and data infrastructure

## Product Direction

- Start with the landing experience and learning model.
- Expand into class management and agent orchestration.
- Add assessment, certification, and student tracking.
- Grow into a full university operating system.

## Closing

AIcademica is not just an assistant.

It is the operating model for a university built around agents, mastery, and real learning.
